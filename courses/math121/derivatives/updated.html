Updated Differentiation Activity

This general differentiation activity is meant for practicing differentiation rules: its generated solutions will not be simplified via algebra or trigonometric identitites - only differentiation rules will be used. For instance, if the question asks for the derivative of the product of two polynomials, the product rule will be used. If it asks for the derivative of \( \frac{\sin(x)}{\cos(x)} \), it will not simplify this to \( \tan(x) \) and then differentiate - it will use the quotient rule. Choose the elements you want to be involved in the problem for practice (it defaults to polynomials and the sum/difference rules). Excuse the occasionally excessive brackets, an artifact of the automation.
