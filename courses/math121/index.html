Welcome to the supplementary page for MATH 121: Calculus I. Here you will find some additional materials and links.

To succeed in Calculus I, you must be very solid on the foundational precalculus skills found here: Precalculus Review. If any of the precalculus activities give you persistent difficulty after attempting them, it may be advisable to take an upgrading course before taking MATH 121.
