Change of Base Activity

Change of base (not to be confused with Ace of Base) involves repeated divison by the target base.

Although this would technically work when converting from a smaller base \(b_1\) to a larger base \(b_2\), it is messy. Since we'll be focusing on conversions to and from decimal (base-10) to binary (base-2) or quinary (base-5), we will simply adopt a simpler strategy when converting to decimal that makes use of our existing familiarity with the decimal system.
