Welcome to my website (permanently under construction)!

Inspired by the totally radical math education programming of the 90s. Because I never grew out of that.

Clearly math education is my primary interest, but I also like number theory and graph theory. My favourite open problems are the Erdős–Faber–Lovász conjecture, and Carmichael's totient function conjecture.
