Basic Cake

Ingredients:

3 cups flour
1 tbsp baking powder

1 cup butter
2 cups sugar
4 eggs
2 tbsp vanilla
1.5 cups milk

1 cup butter
3.5 cups icing sugar
6-8 tbsp milk
1 tbsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

This makes a basic white cake that tastes a bit like a sugar cookie. In the above picture chocolate icing (from the chocolate cake recipe) is used.

Dry ingredients: Mix together 3 cups flour, 1 tbsp baking powder, set aside.

Cream together 1 cup butter with 2 cups sugar. Beat in 4 eggs, 1 at a time. Stir in 2 tbsp vanilla.

Mix in dry ingredients, then stir in 1.5 cups milk until smooth.

Bake at 350 F. Recipe makes 24 jumbo cupcakes. Multiply by 0.75 for a standard 2-layer cake recipe. Test with toothpicks for doneness, use flower nails to cook level as appropriate.

Basic Buttercream Icing: Soften 1 cup butter, whip until fluffy, blend in approximately 3.5 cups icing sugar (500g), and enough milk to make spreadable (by the tablespoon, approx. 6-8), 1 tbsp vanilla extract. Can use half shortening and half butter if desired. Spread icing on levelled cakes. Use less milk if making icing for piping.
