Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

2 cups sugar
1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 cups cocoa
1 1/2 tsp baking soda
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt

2 eggs
1 cups milk
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 tbsp vanilla extract
1 cup freshly brewed coffee

1 cup butter
5 cups icing sugar
1 1/2 cups cocoa
2/3 cup milk
1 tbsp vanilla

Instructions:

Dry ingredients: Mix together 2 cups sugar, 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour, 3/4 cups cocoa, 1 1/2 tsp baking soda, 1 1/2 tsp baking powder, 1 tsp salt.

Blend in 2 eggs, 1 cups milk, 1/2 cup vegetable oil, 1 tbsp vanilla extract at medium-high speed for 2.5-3 minutes (the colour of the batter should start to lighten by the end).

Stir in 1 cup freshly brewed coffee thoroughly. Batter will be very thin and soupy.

Bake at 350 F. Recipe makes approximately 1 13X9 cake or 2 9‚Äù round cakes. Test with toothpicks for doneness, use flower nails to cook level as appropriate.

Chocolate Icing: Soften 3/4 - 1 cup butter, whip until fluffy, blend in approximately 5 cups icing sugar and 1 1/2 cups cocoa alternately with 2/3 cup milk and 1 tbsp vanilla. Spread icing on levelled cakes. Use less milk if making icing for piping.
