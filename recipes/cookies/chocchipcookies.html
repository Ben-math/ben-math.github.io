Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

2 ¼ cups flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
¼ tsp salt

1 cup butter
¾ cup brown sugar
¼ cup white sugar
2 packages of vanilla pudding mix (1.5oz each)
2 eggs
1 tbsp vanilla
2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

Dry ingredients: Mix together 2 ¼ cups flour, 1 tsp baking powder, 1 tsp baking soda, ¼ tsp salt, set aside.

Cream together 1 cup softened butter, ¾ cup brown sugar, ¼ cup white sugar.

Beat in 2 packages of vanilla pudding mix (1.5oz each), then 2 eggs, and 1 tbsp vanilla.

Blend in dry ingredients, stir in 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips, and bake at 350F for 10-12 minutes.

I test cookies for doneness by running a butter knife along the top of them: if they have developed a "crust" and don't depress too much, they will finish cooking in the 5 minutes you leave them on the pan after removing from the oven. Then, move them to a cooling rack.
