Oreo Truffles

This recipe is minimally changed from a version I found on allrecipes. The main difference is omitting the decoration.

Ingredients:

1 package (250g) cream cheese
2 8oz packages semi-sweet chocolate
1 family-size package (439g) oreos

Instructions:

Finely crush oreos using a food processor (can be done by hand, but is a lot of work).
Blend crushed oreos together with cream cheese.
Form balls about 1 inch in diameter of this mixture (you may find it easier to do if the mixture is slightly chilled), then place on a cookie sheet lined in parchment paper and place in the freezer.
In a pot, melt the semi-sweet chocolate.
Remove half of the balls from the freezer (to keep the rest cold, as you'll want to work fast before they soften).
The hard part: One at a time, toss a ball into the pot of melted chocolate, roll it around until coated with a pair of forks, then gently lift it out, let excess chocolate drip off, and place on a cookie sheet lined in parchment paper.
Repeat with the second half of the balls. If you don't work quickly enough, you may have to re-freeze the balls and/or re-melt the chocolate (as they will get softer and stiffer respectively, which makes coating them harder).
Place in fridge to set for an hour, then serve!
